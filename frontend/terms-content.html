1. DEFINITIONS

"Platform" - The Chai coffee grove tokenization platform, including website, mobile applications, smart contracts, and all related services.

"Farmer" - A coffee grove owner who registers their grove on the Platform for tokenization and funding.

"Investor" - A person or entity who purchases grove tokens to receive a share of harvest revenues.

"Grove" - A coffee plantation or farm registered on the Platform for tokenization.

"Token" - A digital asset representing fractional ownership in a grove's revenue stream, issued on the Hedera blockchain.

"Harvest Revenue" - Income generated from the sale of coffee produced by a tokenized grove.

"Milestone Funding" - The structured release of investor funds to farmers based on grove development stages (40% upfront, 30% maintenance, 30% harvest).

"Platform Fee" - A 3% fee charged on fund disbursements to farmers (not on investor contributions).

"Smart Contract" - Self-executing code on the Hedera blockchain that automates platform functions.

"USDC" - USD Coin, the stablecoin used for all platform transactions.

2. PLATFORM OVERVIEW

2.1 Purpose

The Chai Platform connects coffee farmers with investors through blockchain-based tokenization, enabling:

  • Farmers to access funding for grove operations
  • Investors to earn returns from coffee harvest revenues
  • Transparent, secure, and automated financial transactions
  • Sustainable agricultural development and fair trade practices

2.2 Core Services

  • Grove registration and verification
  • Token creation and sales management
  • Milestone-based funding distribution
  • Harvest tracking and revenue distribution
  • Real-time monitoring and reporting
  • Administrative oversight and compliance

2.3 Technology Infrastructure

  • Built on Hedera Hashgraph blockchain
  • Smart contracts for automated execution
  • USDC stablecoin for all transactions
  • Secure wallet integration (HashPack, Blade, etc.)
  • IoT sensors and satellite monitoring (where available)

KEY HIGHLIGHTS

For Farmers:

  • Milestone Funding: 40% upfront, 30% maintenance, 30% harvest
  • Revenue Share: Keep 60% of harvest revenue
  • Platform Fee: Only 3% on disbursements (not on total revenue)
  • Requirements: 2-year grove history, buyer relationships, quality standards
  • Obligations: Monthly reporting, transparent accounting, grove maintenance

For Investors:

  • Revenue Share: Receive 40% of harvest revenue
  • No Fees: Zero fees on token purchases or distributions
  • Protection: 10 layers of investor protection mechanisms
  • Transparency: Real-time grove monitoring and blockchain records
  • Expected Returns: 12-18% annually (not guaranteed)
  • Risks: Agricultural, market, operational, and technology risks

Investor Protection Mechanisms:

  1. Pre-Tokenization Verification - Only proven groves accepted
  2. Guaranteed Offtake Agreements - Pre-arranged buyers
  3. Insurance & Risk Pooling - Up to 80% coverage
  4. Diversification Tools - Spread risk across groves
  5. Alternative Revenue Streams - Carbon credits, ecosystem services
  6. Smart Contract Safeguards - Automated protection
  7. Secondary Market Liquidity - Exit options
  8. Transparency & Monitoring - Real-time tracking
  9. Legal Framework - Enforceable rights
  10. Community Support - Cooperative model
